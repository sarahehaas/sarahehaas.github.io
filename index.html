About

I'm not just a content designer who learned to write buttons. I think in systems.

For over twenty years I've been doing the same thing in different rooms — finding the signal, making it clear, and getting the right people to the other side of complexity. Newsrooms. Mobile apps. Complex product systems. AI.

I'm not just a content designer who learned to write buttons. I think in systems, ask better questions, and care as much about the team making the product as the person using it.

I work at the intersection of content, systems thinking, and product strategy. I care about the flows, the friction, the moment someone almost gives up — and what it takes to get them through. That instinct came from journalism. It never left.

Language is a design tool. I know how to use it.
